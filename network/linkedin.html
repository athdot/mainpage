LinkedIn - Social Media in the Workplace
   Linkedin is product of the times we live in and builds off of ones resume, in a unique fashion. Never before has there been a way to market someone so casually, while still reaching those who are so inclined. Not only does it show your qualifications, your education, and more, but it also shows who you know – who you are connected to, and what your network is. Linkedin is essential for anyone looking for a job, and is highly recommended. By creating my own profile, I was able to create a marketable image – one for employers to see and one to gather the attention of just the right people. I also found myself in a place with more self retrospection, thinking of just what makes me attractive.
